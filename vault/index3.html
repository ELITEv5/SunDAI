What is SunDAI?
▼

pSunDAI is a $1-pegged stablecoin backed by PLS — built entirely on-chain with no owner, no admin keys, and no upgrades ever possible.

You lock PLS as collateral at at least 150% of the value you mint (a CDP). Deposit $150 of PLS, mint up to $100 pSunDAI. Your collateral stays locked until you repay.

A 0.5% annual stability fee accrues on debt. If PLS drops and your ratio falls below 110%, liquidators can partially repay your debt and claim discounted PLS.

Flash crash immune: Price drops shorter than 4 hours are completely ignored. Real drops take 4+ hours to confirm, then step 5% every 30 min. Your vault cannot be flash-liquidated.

How does the oracle work?

Median price from five PulseX pairs (DAI v1/v2, USDC v1/v2, USDT) via TWAP + spot. Moves under 3% accepted instantly. Larger moves need 4 hours confirmation (drops) or 1 hour (pumps), then step 5%/10% per 30 min. Flash crashes are completely ignored.

Manual poke has a 30-minute cooldown. The vault always falls back to the last valid price — you can always repay and withdraw.

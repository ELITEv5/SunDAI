What is SunDAI?
▼

pSunDAI is a $1-pegged stablecoin backed by PLS — built on-chain with no owner, no admin keys, and no upgrades ever possible.

You lock PLS as collateral at at least 150% of the value you mint. A 0.5% annual stability fee accrues on your debt. If PLS drops and your ratio falls below 110%, liquidators can claim your PLS at a 2–5% discount.

Flash crash protected: The oracle ignores price drops shorter than 4 hours. Real drops step in 5% increments every 30 min after a 4-hour confirmation. No flash liquidations possible.

How does this work?

The easiest way to start. Enter the USD value of PLS you want to deposit. The contract deposits your PLS and mints pSunDAI in a single transaction. Always available — no system health restriction.

Your vault opens at 155% collateral ratio. This is hardcoded in the contract — the 5% buffer above the 150% minimum ensures rounding in the blockchain math never causes a revert. To mint at exactly 150%, use Deposit + Mint separately below.

Deposit $155 of PLS → receive $100.00 pSunDAI → ratio: 155%

What is the oracle?

The oracle feeds PLS/USD price from 5 PulseX pairs (DAI v1/v2, USDC v1/v2, USDT) using a TWAP + spot median. Price changes under 3% accept instantly. Larger moves require 4 hours to confirm downward or 1 hour upward, then step in 5% or 10% increments every 30 min.

Manual poke has a 30-minute cooldown. The vault always uses the last valid price as fallback, so repay and withdraw always work.

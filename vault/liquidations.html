How Liquidations Work ▼

  • Liquidation Trigger: Vaults below 110% health ratio can be liquidated
  • Minimum Repay: 20% of total debt (automatically calculated)
  • Dutch Auction Bonus: Starts at 2% and grows to 5% over 3 hours. Earlier liquidators get a smaller bonus but face less competition. Wait longer for a bigger reward, but risk someone else doing it first.
  • Cooldown: 10-minute cooldown between liquidations of the same vault
  • Process: Two-step transaction
    • Step 1: Approve pSunDAI spending (one-time per session)
    • Step 2: Execute liquidation and receive PLS reward
  • Interest Buffer: Dashboard automatically adds 1% buffer to handle debt growth during transaction
  • Auto-Rescan: Dashboard refreshes every 2 minutes to find new opportunities

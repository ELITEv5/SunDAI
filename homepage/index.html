SunPLS — PLS-Native Autonomous Monetary Asset

SunPLS Vault

Algorithmic CDP · PulseChain

Mint SunPLS by depositing PLS as collateral. Unlike dollar-pegged stablecoins, SunPLS targets its own internal equilibrium price R — defended by a feedback controller that automatically adjusts the borrowing rate every hour. No peg promise. No governance. Pure math.

How it works

When SunPLS trades above R, the controller raises the borrowing rate — discouraging new minting and pulling price down. When SunPLS trades below R, the rate falls — making borrowing cheap and encouraging new supply. Arbitrageurs can always redeem SunPLS directly against vault collateral at the R price, creating a hard price floor. Dutch-auction liquidations enforce solvency at all times.

SunPLS vs. SunDAI

SunDAI is collateralized against PLS and targets $1 USD value. SunPLS is also collateralized against PLS but is not pegged to USD — it targets a self-defined internal equilibrium price, making it an independent monetary asset denominated in PLS rather than dollars.
